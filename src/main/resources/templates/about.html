  • So, what's a recommender system?

    A RS is an intelligent system that analyze user behaviour and preferences to make personalized recommendation for products, services, or content. It's commonly used in e-commerce, entertainment and social media.

  • How do recommender systems works?

    Recommender systems use algorithms to analyze user data, such as browsing history, purchase history, ratings, and reviews, to identify patterns and make personalized recommendations. There are different types of recommender systems, including content-based, collaborative filtering, and hybrid approaches.

  • Why do I need a recommender system for my application?

    Save movies, tvs, anime, games, books and follow new friends. But we will just collect your data, filter it and recommend you similar content.

  • What kind of data do I need to build a recommender system?

    To build a recommender system, you typically need user data and content data. User data includes information about the user's behavior, preferences, and bio provided at sign up or profile modifying. Content data includes information about the movies, tvs, book that you mark, review and save.
